Anywayy!! Here's how to make it!!

Ingredients

  • 1L milk
  • 8 egg yolks
  • 200g sugar
  • some salt
  • vanilla

For the foam things:

  • 8 egg whites
  • some salt
  • 50g sugar
  • vanilla

For decorations:

  • chocolate
  • caramel sauce
  • other toppings

Preparation

Idrk how to make this, my mom makes it lol anyway

  1. Separate the egg yolks from the egg whites
  2. Bring milk to boil
  3. Mix the egg whites with salt and sugar
  4. Add vanilla
  5. Add the foam to the milk
  6. Mix the egg yolks with sugar and other stuff
  7. Put the foam on the vanilla yolk sauce thing
  8. Serve
