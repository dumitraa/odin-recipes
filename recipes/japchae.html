How to make Japchae?

First, of all, what is Japchae?

Japchae is a savory and slightly sweet dish of stir-fried glass noodles and vegetables that is popular in Korean cuisine. It's extremely delicious! My grandma used to make it to me before going to war

Anyway! Here's how to make it!

Ingredients

  • glass noodles
  • vegetables (mushrooms, carrots, onions, egg, bell peppers,...)
  • meat
  • sesame seeds
  • sesame oil

For the sauce:

  • soy sauce
  • honey

Preparation

Before I start, I'm going to tell you that this dish is quite versatile so you can add almost any vegetable and/or meat you want. This is just how I make it.

  1. Bring water to boil and add the glass noodles in
  2. Fry the vegetables and/or meat from lightest color to darkest, don't change the oil
  3. Make the sauce by mixing 6 tablespoons of soy sauce and 3-4 tablespoons of honey
  4. Throw out the oil, put the sauce in along with the noodles
  5. After 10 seconds, add the vegetables and/or meat
  6. Add sesame oil and sesame seeds for more flavor
  7. Serve
